My Thoughts

Oh, so you thought I was only going to write about feature length films? You were sorely mistaken my friend!

"On Your Mark" might just be a short music video, but it is gorgeous! The story is adorable. The animation is beautiful. What else do you need me to tell you? Also, have I mentioned there's a little angel girl involved? Because there is! And she has wings and can fly! And these two guys come to her rescue and set her free, and I love it!

Look at her...so free, so happy... We sould all aspire to be more like her! Also the short green hair? What a look! How cute!

This is one of the first films I watched when I was trying to watch every single thing that Ghibli has ever produced. It holds a special place in my heart, and it's really good! Like all the othere things on this website! WOW!
