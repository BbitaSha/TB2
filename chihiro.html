My Thoughts

So, "Spirited Away" is one of the most well known Ghibli movies. It has won an Oscar. It's a big deal. We get it. But do we, though?

The world building for this movie is incredible! I just want to live in the spirit world and sit on the balcony of the bath house and look over the ocean while eating a big ole bread roll!

It is heart-warming and heart-breaking, and I absolutely love it! I love that there aren't any real villains, that the bad guys are all kind of okay in the end. I don't know if that's the most realistic world view, but it's nice, and it makes sense in the context of the movie.

I also love that Chihiro doesn't have any special powers or anything of that kind. She's a normal, regular girl who's kind of average at most things, because she's still a kid, and that's what kids are like!

How great! What a lovely story! What a perfectly appreciated movie! Can you believe it? A good movie being recognized for being a good movie? Sensational!
